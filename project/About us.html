ABOUT VENDITION

Vendition Agencies Pvt. Ltd. is a private limited company incorporated as a result of M/S Lift and Shift Pvt. Ltd. and M/S AKV International deciding to take the SOLE DISTRIBUTORSHIP of KMP Brand in India. The Directors have been in the business of Material Handling at major ports and container stations throughout India, having their own fleet of various material handling equipment and have been in the business of Export of quality Trailer parts for more than 25 years to Australia having developed and exported nearly 300 components to reputed OEM's and distributors in Australia. Also, having been associated with the Earthmoving Parts trade, the Directors have together pooled their resources and experience to form Vendition Agencies Pvt.Ltd. to exclusively market the KMP brand of parts in India.

ABOUT KMP BRAND

KM PRODUCTS EUROPE LTD was established in 1993 for the purposes of creating a reliable supplier of branded, quality, aftermarket replacement parts for Earthmoving Equipment and heavy duty Diesel Engines. The company’s main focus is KMP Brand ® engine components and replacement parts suitable for Caterpillar, Cummins, Detroit Diesel and Komatsu which are marketed under the KMP Brand ® name. KMP Brand is a global company renowned for supplying high quality engine parts suitable for heavy duty Caterpillar®,Komatsu®, Cummins® and Detroit Diesel® engines, recognised for exceptional quality backed with dedicated service and support.With extensive knowledge of heavy duty and agricultural machinery and a commitment to providing only the highest quality, affordable, replacement parts, KMP is one of the leading heavy duty engine part suppliers and agricultural machinery part suppliers in the world.
